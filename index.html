Privacy Policy — Ordering Agent

Last updated: December 16, 2025

Who we are

Ordering Agent (“we”, “us”) is an app provided by Neatly Brands. This policy explains how we handle personal information when you use our app and any Meta integrations (Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp).

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the app:

  • Account/contact info you provide (e.g., name, email, phone number)
  • Content you submit in the app (e.g., messages, orders, requests)
  • Meta platform data if you connect via Meta (e.g., identifiers, business/page info, messaging metadata/content as needed for the integration)

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the app’s features
  • Support messaging/integration functionality you enable
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell your personal information.

How we share information

We may share information:

  • With Meta as required to provide the integration you enabled
  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, logs/analytics) who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations
  • If required by law or to protect safety, rights, and security

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. We may delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.

Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No system is 100% secure, but we work to prevent unauthorized access and misuse.

Your choices and deletion requests

You can request access or deletion of your data by emailing: neatlybrands@gmail.com. If you use Meta login or messaging features, you can also manage some permissions through your Meta account settings.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your location). We do not knowingly collect data from children.
